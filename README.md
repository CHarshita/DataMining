•	Project Description:
TruthX-Lite is a lightweight implementation inspired by the TruthX framework, designed to reduce hallucinations in Large Language Models (LLMs) by improving their truthfulness during response generation. The system works by analyzing the model’s internal hidden representations and identifying a “truthful direction” that distinguishes correct and incorrect outputs. By applying inference-time intervention (ITI), the model’s internal states are adjusted across selected layers to bias outputs toward more factual responses. This approach enhances the reliability of LLMs without retraining the entire model, making it efficient and practical for real-world applications.
